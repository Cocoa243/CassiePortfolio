ABOUT ME

On March 19th, 1994, I embarked on a plane called “life”. I was born in Brussels, the capital of Belgium and the centre of European institutions. I grew up in a multicultural environment. I have a Congolese (DRC) background, I grew up in the multicultural neighbourhood of Saint-Gilles, in the South of Brussels and I have always been surrounded by people from different ethnicities.
This environment must have had big impact of my study choices and my love for languages. I studied at Université Catholique de Louvain (Louvain-la-Neuve, Belgium). I have a Bachelor Degree in Modern Languages and Literatures, option Spanish/English. I also have a Master’s Degree in Multilingual Communications (English/Spanish), specialisation “business language”. I also took Portuguese classes , basic level. During my studies, I had the opportunity to do a 5-month Erasmus internship in Madrid (Spain) in Customer Service.

My career began when I was still a student. I worked as a Receptionist and Administrative Employee in a social & environmental development company and also in a language online teaching development company. Working as a student allowed to do something I really enjoy : travelling. My favourite trips where to Kinshasa, Mexico, Miami , Morocco and Lisbon.
I graduated in June 2018 and I started working as an Administrative Employee at Management Centre Europe (Brussels, Belgium). After 10 month, I have been promoted to Marketing Assistant. This job consist of managing the social media accounts of the company, setting up Google Ads campaigns, create promotional material and other digital marketing tasks.
As many companies, Management Centre Europe has been impacted by the corona virus crisis consequences. For that reason , I find myself unemployed since April 2020. I took unemployed moment to maximise my time and boost my skills. As I live in the Dutch-speaking part of Belgium since 2017, I finally had time to take Dutch classes. I thus speaks now French, English, Spanish, Portuguese, Lingala and Dutch. I also decided to join the 6-week Cheetah Codes Bootcamp to learn the basics of coding and be able to build my own website from scratch. I would love to combine my marketing skills and basic coding skills on my next job.

THE 4 M

The project “The 4 M” is a lifestyle blog. Why this name? M is the first letter “Mengato” (my last name). The number 4 comes from the fact that my sisters and I are 4. This blog is about love, fun and sisterhood. It gives tips ideas on how to spend quality time with ones loved ones.
It was created using HTML 5 and CSS.

I FEEL GOOD

The project “I FEEL GOOD” is also a lifestyle blog. It focuses on 4 aspects that contributes to feeling good: physical health, mental health, emotional health and spiritual health.
It was created using HTML 5, CSS, Bootstrap and JavaScript.
